Why should you study science?

Some of you may not like science and may be thinking "Why in the world would I want to know all this?". Well let me give you a few reasons why. And maybe you can reconsider.

  • Many recruiters like science graduates
  • You can learn how humankind impacts the climate
  • Learning how the earth works helps you to plan and survive natural disasteres like earthquakes, tornadoes, and tsunamis
