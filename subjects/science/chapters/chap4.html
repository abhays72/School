4 - Sorting Materials Into Groups

What are Materials?

Materials are anything that is made up of matter.

What is Classification?

The process of grouping objects based on known criteria is called classification.

Why do we need Classification?

We classify things for two reasons:

  1. To help us organize things
  2. To allow the systematic study of things

Based on what criteria are materials classified?

Appearance

Checking if the material has lustre, if it reflects light etc.

Hardness

Checking if the material is hard or soft.

Soluble or Insoluble

If the material is solid we check if the material is soluble or insoluble in water.

Miscible or Immiscible

If the material is liquid we check if the material is miscible or immiscible in water.

Density

Checking if the material floats or sinks in water.

Transparency

Checking if the material is transparent, translucent, or opaque.
